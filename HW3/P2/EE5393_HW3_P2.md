# Problem 2: Transforming Probabilities

The homework asks for two parts:

- **(a)** Starting from source probabilities \(\{0.4,0.5\}\), implement
  \(0.8881188\), \(0.2119209\), and \(0.5555555\) using **AND** and **NOT** gates.
- **(b)** Starting from source probability set \(\{0.5\}\), implement the given binary probabilities with logic. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Background

In stochastic computing:

$$
P(A \land B)=P(A)P(B)
$$

and

$$
P(\neg A)=1-P(A)
$$

I will also use OR as shorthand, with the understanding that it is implemented using only AND and NOT:

$$
A \lor B=\neg(\neg A \land \neg B)
$$

---

# (a) Implement probabilities using \(S=\{0.4,0.5\}\)

Let:

$$
P(A=1)=0.4,\qquad P(B=1)=0.5
$$

From these we can immediately generate the following useful probabilities:

$$
0.6=\neg A
$$

$$
0.2=A \land B
$$

$$
0.3=\neg A \land \neg B
$$

$$
0.7=\neg(0.3)
$$

$$
0.8=\neg(0.2)
$$

$$
0.1=0.2 \land 0.5
$$

$$
0.9=\neg(0.1)
$$

These will be reused below.

---

## (a)(i) Implement \(0.8881188\)

Define the following intermediate probabilities:

$$
p_1 = 0.4 \lor 0.3 = 0.4 + 0.3 - (0.4)(0.3) = 0.58
$$

$$
p_2 = 0.8 \lor p_1 = 0.8 + 0.2(0.58) = 0.916
$$

$$
p_3 = 0.3 \lor p_2 = 0.3 + 0.7(0.916) = 0.9412
$$

$$
p_4 = 0.5 \land p_3 = 0.5(0.9412) = 0.4706
$$

$$
p_5 = 0.3 \lor p_4 = 0.3 + 0.7(0.4706) = 0.62942
$$

$$
p_6 = 0.7 \land p_5 = 0.7(0.62942) = 0.440594
$$

$$
z = 0.8 \lor p_6 = 0.8 + 0.2(0.440594) = 0.8881188
$$

Thus, \(0.8881188\) is implemented exactly.

---

## (a)(ii) Implement \(0.2119209\)

Define:

$$
q_1 = 0.1 \land 0.5 = 0.05
$$

$$
q_2 = 0.9 \lor q_1 = 0.9 + 0.1(0.05) = 0.905
$$

$$
q_3 = 0.4 \land q_2 = 0.4(0.905) = 0.362
$$

$$
q_4 = 0.3 \land q_3 = 0.3(0.362) = 0.1086
$$

$$
q_5 = 0.5 \lor q_4 = 0.5 + 0.5(0.1086) = 0.5543
$$

$$
q_6 = 0.3 \lor q_5 = 0.3 + 0.7(0.5543) = 0.68801
$$

$$
q_7 = 0.6 \land q_6 = 0.6(0.68801) = 0.412806
$$

$$
q_8 = 0.5 \lor q_7 = 0.5 + 0.5(0.412806) = 0.706403
$$

$$
z = 0.3 \land q_8 = 0.3(0.706403) = 0.2119209
$$

Thus, \(0.2119209\) is implemented exactly.

---

## (a)(iii) Implement \(0.5555555\)

Define:

$$
r_1 = 0.9 \lor 0.7 = 0.9 + 0.1(0.7) = 0.97
$$

$$
r_2 = 0.2 \land r_1 = 0.2(0.97) = 0.194
$$

$$
r_3 = 0.3 \land r_2 = 0.3(0.194) = 0.0582
$$

$$
r_4 = 0.5 \lor r_3 = 0.5 + 0.5(0.0582) = 0.5291
$$

$$
r_5 = 0.7 \land r_4 = 0.7(0.5291) = 0.37037
$$

$$
r_6 = 0.3 \land r_5 = 0.3(0.37037) = 0.111111
$$

$$
z = 0.5 \lor r_6 = 0.5 + 0.5(0.111111) = 0.5555555
$$

Thus, \(0.5555555\) is implemented exactly.

# (b) Implement binary probabilities using \(S=\{0.5\}\)

Let \(x_1,x_2,\dots,x_7\) be independent stochastic streams with

$$
P(x_i=1)=0.5
$$

The key rule is:

- bit **1** \(\rightarrow\) OR with a fresh \(0.5\) stream
- bit **0** \(\rightarrow\) AND with a fresh \(0.5\) stream

---

## (b)(i) \(0.1011111_2\)

Use:

$$
\begin{aligned}
z_7 &= x_7 \\
z_6 &= x_6 \lor z_7 \\
z_5 &= x_5 \lor z_6 \\
z_4 &= x_4 \lor z_5 \\
z_3 &= x_3 \lor z_4 \\
z_2 &= x_2 \land z_3 \\
z_1 &= x_1 \lor z_2
\end{aligned}
$$

with output

$$
z=z_1
$$

Then

$$
P(z)=0.7421875
$$

and

$$
0.1011111_2=0.7421875
$$

---

## (b)(ii) \(0.1101111_2\)

Use:

$$
\begin{aligned}
z_7 &= x_7 \\
z_6 &= x_6 \lor z_7 \\
z_5 &= x_5 \lor z_6 \\
z_4 &= x_4 \lor z_5 \\
z_3 &= x_3 \land z_4 \\
z_2 &= x_2 \lor z_3 \\
z_1 &= x_1 \lor z_2
\end{aligned}
$$

with output

$$
z=z_1
$$

Then

$$
P(z)=0.8671875
$$

and

$$
0.1101111_2=0.8671875
$$

---

## (b)(iii) \(0.1010111_2\)

Use:

$$
\begin{aligned}
z_7 &= x_7 \\
z_6 &= x_6 \lor z_7 \\
z_5 &= x_5 \lor z_6 \\
z_4 &= x_4 \land z_5 \\
z_3 &= x_3 \lor z_4 \\
z_2 &= x_2 \land z_3 \\
z_1 &= x_1 \lor z_2
\end{aligned}
$$

with output

$$
z=z_1
$$

Then

$$
P(z)=0.6796875
$$

and

$$
0.1010111_2=0.6796875
$$
