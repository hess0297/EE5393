## Chemical Reaction Network
- This CRN generates Fibonacci numbers using intermediate carrier species $A_n$ and $B_n$.

- The initialization reaction
  $$
  S \to A_1 + B_1 + X_1 + X_2
  $$
  sets \(X_1 = X_2 = 1\).

- At each stage, the reactions
  $$
  A_n \to B_{n+1} + X_{n+2}
  $$
  $$
  B_n \to A_{n+1} + B_{n+1} + X_{n+2}
  $$
  pass information forward and produce the next output species.

- The carrier populations obey
  $$
  A_{n+1} = B_n, \qquad B_{n+1} = A_n + B_n
  $$
  so they evolve according to the Fibonacci recursion.

- Therefore, the number of molecules of $X_{n+2}$ produced at each stage follows the Fibonacci sequence.
